**Rationale:**
- Each row is an observation/diagnosis/event about a patient, at a time, with a code.
- SPHN/AIDAVA `Observation` is designed to be the central entity connecting patient, code, and date.
- Alternative: [`/Condition`](https://biomedit.ch/rdf/sphn-ontology/AIDAVA/Condition), if you know all codes are conditions. But "Observation" is more general and safely encompasses both diagnoses and other coded events.
